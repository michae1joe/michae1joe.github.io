Soundtrack

The music of Tron.
The music is one of the most important elements of this franchise. Tron Legacy's score was written by Daft Punk, and featured Electronica mixed with a live orchestra. They created one of the most unique and strong movie scores of its time. People like myself remembered the soundtrack before the movie.
For Ares, the soundtrack will be done by Nine Inch Nails. It will be interesting to see how their style and experience will affect this movie.
