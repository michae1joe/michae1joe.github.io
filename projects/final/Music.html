Idol Groups

This is the most noteable element of splatoon music. Each game has an idol group. They do the map announcements and even live splatfest performances. Each group has its own unique sound. Splatoon's group is the Squid Sisters a J-Pop group. Splatoon 2 features Off the Hook a Hip-hop duo. Splatoon 3, the most unique by far, has Deep Cut. Each member brings a different culture of music. We have Shiver with traditional japenese music, Frye with Indian/East asian, and Big Man with a brazilian sound.

In-Game Bands

Splatoon has in game bands with albums and member descriptions in all. They display a variety of genres heard in different areas of the game. Certant bands play over multiplayer battles, while others are in the singleplayer modes. The one featured in this picture is Yoko and the Gold Bazookas. They play ska-style music.

Physical Media

The downside of being a Splatoon fan is that most merchandise is locked to japan. There have been releases of all the soundtracks though! Its just hard to get and most likely written in japenese...

Most of the music from Splatoon 2 and 3 is currently available on the Nintendo Music app.
