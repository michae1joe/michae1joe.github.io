Splatoon is Nintendo's unique take on a third person shooter. The gameplay consists of many modes from solo to multiplayer.

Core Gameplay
  • Players are made up of Inklings or Octolings. These are humanoid characters who can transform into squids/octopi at will. Which is part of its core gameplay mechaniics.
  • Players use ink-based weapons to cover the ground and splat opponents. Your ink is a visual indicator of owned turf. Its also your main movement option. Swimming in your own ink (in squid/octo form) lets you move faster, hide, and refill ink.
    • Game Modes
  • Turf War, Cover the most ground with your team's ink in 3-minute matches.
  • Ranked Battles; Splat Zones, Control designated areas of turf. Tower Control, Ride a moving tower into the enemy’s base. Rainmaker, Carry a powerful weapon to the enemy’s goal. Clam Blitz, Collect and throw clams into the opponent’s basket.
  • Salmon Run; Team up to fight waves of Salmonids and collect Golden Eggs. This co-op mode is often challenging, with rotating weapons and hazards.
  • Story Mode; Single-player missions that offer platforming challenges and boss fights. Use your resources and learn the gameplay in depth.
  • Paid DLC; The first one, Octo Expansion, features a unique take on the singleplayer mode that would continue this format in Splatoon 3. The most recent one, Side Order, is a Rouguelite. You acend a tower and risk a reset by a single death. In each DLC you face bosses and unique missions.
    • Weapons & Gear
  • Main Weapons: Your guns, they consist of recognizable classes like snipers, as well as unique ones like rollers.
  • Sub Weapons: Your utility, these are Bombs, beacons, traps, etc.
  • Special Weapons: Aka your Ultamate, these are powerful abilities you use after inking a specific amount of turf.
  • Gear: Clothes have ability slots that help boost your stats.
